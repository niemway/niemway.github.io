About

  Hi! I'm Nicholas, a student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, currently studying towards a B.S. in Computer Science and German. Currently, I'm spending a year in Freiburg im Breisgau, Germany, at the Albert-Ludwigs-Universität.

  I'm passionate about art, particularly exploring how it can intersect with technology. I love experimenting with ways interactivity can enhance education and communication.

  In my free time, I am an avid board game player, painter, reader and public transportation enthusiast.
