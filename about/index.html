About

Hi, I’m Nicholas! I'm a Computer Science and German student at the University of Michigan. I’m passionate about the intersection of art and technology, and I love building things that are both functional and beautiful.

My journey started in High School with game development, where I discovered how code can be art, creating immersive worlds and experiences otherwise impossible. That curiosity has since expanded into web development, VR/AR, creative coding, and board game design. I’m especially interested in how interactive experiences can be used for education, storytelling, and community building.

When I’m not at a keyboard, you’ll likely find me at a board game table, painting, reading, or exploring the public transit system of a new city. I’m a firm believer that the best ideas come from playful experimentation and cross‑disciplinary thinking.

I’m currently looking for opportunities where I can combine my technical skills with my love for design and human‑centered experiences. If you have an interesting project or just want to chat, please reach out!
