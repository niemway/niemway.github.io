The Ballroom Problem is a multiplayer game where two players try to murder the other in a dark ballroom. Blend in with the guests to sneak up on your opponent.
Requires at least one controller, but is best with two (one player can use a keyboard).

Made for the WolverineSoft 2023 Mega Jam with Musa Haydar and Jacob Robinson, where it won first place.
